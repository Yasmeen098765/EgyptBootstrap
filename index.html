Discover Egypt

Experience the magic of ancient wonders and modern marvels with our premium Egypt tours. From the Pyramids of Giza to Alexandria's Mediterranean charm, we craft unforgettable journeys. Choose from day excursions to 10-day luxury packages with 5-star accommodations, private guides, and 24/7 concierge service.

Egypt's Must-See Destinations

Home to the legendary Pyramids and Sphinx, the Giza Plateau is the only surviving wonder of the ancient world. Our tours include exclusive access to pyramid interiors, the Solar Boat Museum, and spectacular sound-and-light shows. Evening camel rides offer breathtaking sunset views of these 4,500-year-old monuments.

Known as the world's greatest open-air museum, Luxor boasts the temples of Karnak and Luxor, the Valley of the Kings (including Tutankhamun's tomb), and the magnificent Temple of Hatshepsut. Our Nile cruise packages feature guided tours with Egyptologists, hot air balloon rides at dawn, and authentic Nubian cultural experiences.

From the world-class diving in Sharm El-Sheikh to the spiritual serenity of St. Catherine's Monastery at Mount Sinai, the Sinai offers diverse experiences. Our adventure packages include Red Sea snorkeling, desert safaris, and climbing Mount Moses for sunrise. The coastal town of Dahab offers perfect windsurfing conditions and laidback beach vibes.

Egypt Travel News

Grand Egyptian Museum Nears Completion

Wed, June 12th 2024

 

The long-awaited Grand Egyptian Museum near the Giza Pyramids is set to partially open this fall, showcasing over 50,000 artifacts including the complete Tutankhamun collection. This monumental project will be the world's largest archaeological museum, featuring state-of-the-art conservation labs and immersive virtual reality experiences.

Luxury Nile Cruises Resume Full Operations

Mon, May 20th 2024

 

After extensive renovations, Egypt's iconic Nile cruise ships are welcoming visitors with enhanced safety measures and new luxury amenities. The 7-night journeys between Luxor and Aswan now feature gourmet dining, onboard Egyptologists, and exclusive access to temple sites during cooler evening hours.

Red Sea Resorts Break Visitor Records

Fri, April 5th 2024

 

Sharm El Sheikh and Hurghada have reported their highest visitor numbers since 2010, with European travelers leading the surge. New direct flights and visa-on-arrival policies have made Egypt's world-class diving sites more accessible. Marine conservation programs now allow tourists to participate in coral reef restoration.

Siwa Oasis Named Top Eco-Destination

Tue, March 19th 2024

 

Egypt's remote Siwa Oasis has been recognized by National Geographic as one of 2024's top sustainable travel destinations. The ancient settlement offers unique experiences including salt lake floats, stargazing in the desert, and stays in traditional mud-brick eco-lodges. New solar-powered transportation makes visits more environmentally friendly.
